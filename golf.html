Bobby Weed Golf Design

Unreal Engine · Real-Time Archviz

Real-time environment visualizations built in Unreal Engine for Bobby Weed Golf Design, one of the world's most respected golf course architects. These levels were produced to communicate design intent across proposed course layouts, replacing traditional static renders with fully navigable, real-time flyovers.

These Unreal Engine projects were instrumental in demonstrating the spatial relationships between key course features, such as fairways, greens, and hazards to the course stakeholders. The courses visualized in these projects include Grandfather Country Club in North Carolina and Palma Ceia Golf & Country Club in Florida.
